Bird Friendly Coffee

The Smithsonian Institution's Bird Friendly certification identifies organic coffees from around the world that are grown beneath a shade cover. This provides quality habitat for birds - both migrant and resident - and other organisms. Research at the Smithsonian Migratory Bird Center (SMBC) continues to explore ways that agricultural lands can have environmental value, ultimately linking conservation to the market place.

This map shows Bird Friendly's reach as of 2012. I created it using QGIS and Adobe Illustrator. It was published in Guerilla Cartography's 'Food: An Atlas' in 2013.
